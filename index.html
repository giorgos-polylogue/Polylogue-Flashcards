flashcards

Practice your lesson vocabulary out loud. Simple drill cards with native audio — go through the deck as many times as you like.

How it works
1
Pick a deck
Choose any lesson or conversation topic below. Cards are shuffled each time you start.
2
Tap to reveal
See the English, try to say the Greek out loud, then tap to reveal the answer and hear it spoken.
3
Practice again
When you reach the end, hit "Practice again" to reshuffle and go through the deck again.
Speak out loud before flipping. Don't just think the answer — say it. The goal isn't to finish the deck, it's to build the reflex of speaking Greek. 5–10 minutes a day beats one long session.
