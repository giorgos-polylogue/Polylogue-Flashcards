Practice your lesson vocabulary using spaced repetition — review cards daily and build lasting memory.

How it works
1
Open a lesson
Pick any lesson below. On your first visit you'll see all the cards for that lesson.
2
Tap to reveal
Each card shows English first. Tap to flip and reveal the Greek — and hear it spoken aloud.
3
Rate yourself honestly
After seeing the Greek, rate how well you knew it. Honest ratings make the system work.
4
Come back daily
Cards get scheduled into the future. Each day you only see what's due — fewer cards, better memory.
What the ratings mean
Again You didn't know it. The card comes back again in this session, and is due again tomorrow.
Hard You knew it, but with effort. The card is done for today and comes back in 1–2 days.
Good You knew it well. The card comes back in a few days, then the interval grows each time.
Easy You knew it immediately. The card is pushed far into the future — weeks or more.
The goal is not to finish the deck in one sitting. The goal is to come back daily and work through whatever is due. Over time, cards you know well disappear for weeks. Cards you struggle with keep coming back until they stick. 5–10 minutes a day is enough.
